# Travel India - JavaFX Application

Welcome to the Travel India application, a comprehensive travel planning tool designed using JavaFX. This desktop application is crafted to assist users in exploring, planning, and booking various aspects of their trips within India. Whether you're looking to relax at a beach, hike in the mountains, or explore historical sites, Travel India offers a seamless interface to make your journey easier and more enjoyable.

## Features

**User Authentication:**
Secure user login and registration functionalities ensure that each user's travel plans and bookings are personalized and private.

**Destination Search:**
Users can search for a variety of destinations across India. Each destination includes details about attractions, estimated costs, and exclusive activities that can be explored.

**Accommodation Booking:**
This feature allows users to view and book accommodations tailored to their destination. It includes a range of options from luxury hotels to budget stays, providing information like pricing, type of accommodation, and availability.

**Sightseeing Tours:**
Users can discover and book sightseeing tours. This service fetches available options based on the selected destination and offers a comprehensive list of attractions, helping users to plan their day-wise itinerary.

**Transport Management:**
Manage all your transport needs with options to book transport to the destination and for intra-city travels. Whether it's booking a flight, train, or bus to reach your destination, or arranging local transport like cars and bikes, all can be handled within the app.

**Feedback System:**
After their trip, users are encouraged to provide feedback on their booking experience, accommodations, and any services used. This helps in improving the service quality and user experience.

## Application Components

**Accommodation Service:**
Manages data retrieval and updates for accommodations related to specific destinations. It interacts with the database to fetch the latest available lodging details.

**Booking Service:**
Handles all aspects of booking accommodations and sightseeing options. It stores all booking details in the database, ensuring users can view their booking history and manage current bookings.

**Feedback Page:**
A dedicated interface where users can submit their feedback. This module collects user inputs on various aspects of their travel experience, which helps in enhancing service quality.

**Sightseeing Options Service:**
Provides detailed information about sightseeing activities at various destinations. Users can view and choose from available options and book them directly through the application.

**Transport Module:**
Allows users to book transport options both for reaching their destination and moving within it. This module ensures that users have a seamless connection from their arrival to their departure.

## Usage

Upon launching the application, users are prompted to either log in or register. Once authenticated, the user can access the full range of functionalities from searching for destinations to booking hotels and transport services. Each section of the application provides intuitive navigation and user-friendly interfaces to assist in planning the perfect trip tailored to personal preferences and needs.

Travel India makes it easy to handle all travel arrangements in one place, ensuring that planning your next vacation is as relaxing as the vacation itself.
